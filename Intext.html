WPU Nursing Program

I'm a nursing student at William Paterson University in Wayne, NJ — working toward my Bachelor of Science in Nursing and my RN license. My goal is to work in a hospital and genuinely help people when they need it most.

I'm proudly first-generation. Being the first in my family to earn a college degree isn't just personal — it's bigger than me. My Jamaican roots, my mom, and my brother Francois are all part of why I show up every single day.

Outside academics I'm growing — socially, financially, and as a person. I value authenticity above everything. I stay true to myself, dress with intention, and believe how you carry yourself matters.
